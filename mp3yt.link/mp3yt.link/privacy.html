Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how MP3YT collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to operate the service. This may include:

  • Server logs (IP address, browser type, referring URLs, timestamps) for security and diagnostics.
  • Conversion metadata needed to process your request (e.g., video identifiers).

How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide and improve the service, troubleshoot issues, detect abuse, and secure our platform. We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use essential cookies for basic functionality (such as language or theme preferences). We do not use cookies for targeted advertising.

Data Retention

Conversion-related data is retained only as long as necessary to complete the process and ensure service reliability. Logs are retained for a limited period for security and diagnostics.

Third-Party Services

We may rely on third-party infrastructure providers to deliver the service. These providers are bound by their own privacy and security obligations.

Your Choices

You may choose not to provide certain information, though this may affect the ability to use the service. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not function correctly.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

MP3YT is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@mp3yt.link.
